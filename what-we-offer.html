What We Offer

Plutos Equities delivers intelligent capital forecasting by predicting quarterly financial metrics such as revenue, EPS, and operating income for NASDAQ Top 100 companies. By combining structured and unstructured data through advanced machine learning models, our platform empowers users to anticipate earnings results before official reports are released.

Quarterly Financial Forecasting

Generate accurate forecasts for revenue, earnings per share (EPS), and other financial metrics using historical data, financial statements, and real-time market inputs such as news sentiment and earnings call transcripts.

Key Feature Visualized confidence intervals and prediction breakdowns for informed decision-making.

Customized Dashboards & Predictions

Track your favorite companies, visualize historical and predicted trends, and export forecast results in multiple formats. Personalize your dashboard to match your interests and investment goals.

Key Feature Watchlist integration and export functionality (.csv/.xls).

AI-Powered Inference System

Utilizing models such as Random Forest, LSTM, and BERT-based sentiment analysis, Plutos Equities extracts insight from both numerical and textual data to predict future performance of top companies.

Key Feature Financial predictions based on latest model pipelines and feature engineering from structured/unstructured sources.
